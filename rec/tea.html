Tea

Tea is the elixir of life, fueling the body and invigorating the spirit with every sip.

Ingredients Required

  • 1 black tea bag or 1 teaspoon loose black tea
  • 1 Cup water
  • 1/2 Cup Milk
  • 1-2 teaspoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Ice Cube (optional)

Lets brew it

Step 1:
In a small pot, bring the water to a boil.
Step 2:
Add the tea bag or loose tea to the pot and let steep for 3-5 minutes.
Step 3:
While the tea is steeping, heat the milk in a separate pot or in the microwave until it is warm but not boiling.
Step 4:
Remove the tea bag or strain out the loose tea.
Step 5:
Add the sugar to the tea and stir until dissolved.
Step 6:
Pour the warm milk into the tea and stir.
Step 7:
If you prefer your milk tea cold, add ice cubes to the drink.
