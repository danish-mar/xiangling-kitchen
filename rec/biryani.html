Vegetable biryani

Biryani is the king of all rice dishes.

Ingredients Required

  • 2 Cups basmati rice (rinsed and soaked for 30 Minutes)
  • 2 tablespoon ghee or oil
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 Green chilies (chopped)
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots peas greenBeans cauliflower potatoes) chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 2 tablespoons fried onions (optional)

Lets Make it

Step 1:

Heat the ghee or oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the sliced onion and sauté until golden brown, stirring occasionally.

Step 2:

Add the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, and sauté for another minute.

Step 3:

Add the mixed vegetables and sauté for 5-7 minutes until they are partially cooked.

Step 4:

Add the turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala powder, and salt, and sauté for 2-3 minutes.

Step 5:

Add the yogurt, cilantro leaves, and mint leaves, and mix well.

Step 6:

Add the soaked rice and 3 1/2 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is fully cooked and the water has been absorbed.

Step 7:

Remove from heat and let it sit for 5-10 minutes. Fluff the rice with a fork.

Step 8:

Garnish with fried onions (if using) and serve hot with raita (yogurt sauce) and/or papadum (crispy Indian crackers).
