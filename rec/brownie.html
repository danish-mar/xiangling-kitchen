Make Time : 1H 20Mins

Brownie

The perfect cake for brownie lovers

Ingredients Required

  • 175g unsalted butter
  • 225g dark chocolate
  • 200g caster sugar
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 65g plain flour
  • 50g chopped pecan nuts

Lets Make it

Step 1:
Heat oven to 180C. Butter a 20-25cm cake tin and line with greaseproof paper
Step 2:
Place 175g of the chocolate plus the butter and sugar in a heavy-based pan and heat gently until melted strring occasionally. leave to cool
Step 3:
Whisk the egg yolk into the chocolate mixture then add the flour nuts and the remaining chocolate
Step 4:
Whisk the egg white until they form soft peaks then gently but thoroughly fold into the chocolate mixture.
Step 5:
Pour into the prepared tin and bake in the center of the oven for about 35-40 mins until crusty on top. leave to cool then run a knife around the sides and remove from the tin. Dust with icing sugar and serve warm with custard or ice cream.

Now Enjoy your best ever brownie
