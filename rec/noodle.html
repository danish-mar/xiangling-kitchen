Vegetable Noodles

Noodles: the gift that keeps on slurping

Ingredients Required

  • 8 oz, of your favourite noodles (such as spaghetti or udon)
  • 2 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup of sliced carrots
  • 1 cup of sliced cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sause
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped green onion or cilantro for garnish

Lets Cook it

Step 1:
Cook noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
Step 2:
Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
Step 3:
Add minced garlic and stir-fry for 30 seconds or until fragrant.
Step 4:
Add sliced onion, bell pepper, mushrooms, carrots, and cabbage, and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
Step 5:
Add cooked noodles to the skillet and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes or until the noodles are heated through.
Step 6:
Pour soy sauce over the noodles and toss everything together until the noodles are evenly coated.
Step 7:
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Step 8:
Garnish with chopped green onions or cilantro if desired.
