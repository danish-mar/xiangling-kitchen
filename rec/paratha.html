Make Time : 30 Mins

Aloo paratha

Life is great. Cheese makes it better. Aloo Paratha makes it perfect.

Ingredients Required

  • 2 Cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 2 large potatoes boiled and mashed
  • 1 small onion finely chopped
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/4 tsp garam masala
  • salt (to taste)
  • Oil

Lets Make it

Step 1:
In a large mixing bowl, add the whole wheat flour and a pinch of salt. Mix well.
Step 2:
Gradually add water to the flour mixture and knead to form a smooth dough. Cover the dough and set aside for 10-15 minutes.
Step 3:
In a separate mixing bowl, add the mashed potatoes, cumin seeds, coriander powder, red chilli powder, garam masala powder, amchur, green chillies, coriander leaves, and salt to taste. Mix well.
Step 4:
Divide the dough into equal-sized balls and roll each ball into a small circle.
Step 5:
Place a spoonful of the potato mixture in the centre of the circle and bring the edges of the circle towards the centre, enclosing the filling completely. Press gently to flatten the stuffed ball.
Step 6:
Dust some flour on the stuffed ball and roll it out into a circle of around 6-7 inches in diameter.
Step 7:
Heat a griddle or tawa on medium-high heat. Place the rolled out paratha on the griddle and cook for 1-2 minutes on one side.
Step 8:
Flip the paratha over and cook the other side for 1-2 minutes.
Step 9:
Apply oil or ghee on both sides of the paratha and cook until both sides are golden brown and crispy.
Step 10:
Repeat with the remaining dough and potato mixture to make more parathas.
