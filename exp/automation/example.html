Coffee

Coffee the common thread that brings people together.

Ingredients Required

  • Instant Coffee powder
  • Hot Water
  • Milk (Optional)
  • Sugar or Sweetner (Optional)

Lets Make it

Step 1:
Heat the water in a kettle or on the stolve until it's hot, but not boiling.
Step 2:
Add the coffee: Add 1-2 teaspoon of Nescafe instant coffee powder to a cup.
Step 3:
Add the water: Pour hot water into the cup, stirring the coffee and water together until the coffee is completely dissolved. Use approximately 6-8 ounces of water per cup.
Step 4:
Add milk and sugar (optional): Add milk or cream and sugar or sweetener to taste, if desired.
